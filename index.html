Flood Scenario Methodology

The 2025 flood scenario represents the 1% annual chance floodplain based on Rutgers’ modified statewide FEMA flood hazard data, integrating effective and preliminary FEMA flood maps.

The 2050 flood scenario represents higher flood elevations developed by Rutgers in consultation with NJDEP, reflecting approximately three feet of additional water above the FEMA 1% flood, consistent with New Jersey’s Inland Flood Protection Rule.

These flood scenarios represent water-level–based flooding and do not include rainfall-driven runoff or sewer system impacts.
